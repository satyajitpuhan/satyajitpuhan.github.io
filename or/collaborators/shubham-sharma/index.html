About

Dr. Shubham Sharma is an esteemed postdoctoral researcher at the Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology (MIPT) in Russia, having completed his PhD at NIT Jalandhar. With deep expertise spanning light-front formalisms, his highly cited research illuminates Transverse Momentum-Dependent parton distributions (TMDs) and the multi-dimensional structure of pseudo-scalar mesons.
