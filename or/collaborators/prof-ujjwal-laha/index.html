About

Prof. Ujjwal Laha is a distinguished Professor in the Department of Physics at the National Institute of Technology, Jamshedpur. An expert in Theoretical Physics with an impressive h-index and vast citations, his highly influential work explores quantum scattering, Coulomb-distorted nuclear scattering, and Hamiltonian hierarchies within complex potentials.

Research Interests
Quantum ScatteringCoulomb-Distorted Nuclear ScatteringHamiltonian HierarchiesSupersymmetry-Inspired Factorization
