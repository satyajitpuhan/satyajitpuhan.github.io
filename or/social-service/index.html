Durga Seva

ଦୁର୍ଗା ସେବା — दुर्गा सेवा — Service of Strength & Compassion

A social service initiative founded by Satyajit Puhan to help underprivileged children access quality education by providing books, school supplies, and learning resources. From a small village in Odisha to the world — giving back to where it all started.
