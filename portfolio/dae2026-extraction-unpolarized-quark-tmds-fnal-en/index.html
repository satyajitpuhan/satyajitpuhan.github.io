Extraction of unpolarized quark TMDs of proton from FNAL-E-0605 data using iminuit

69th DAE Symposium on Nuclear Physics

Authors

Abhishek K. P, Reetanshu Pandey, Satyajit Puhan, Suneel Dutt, Harleen Dahiya et al.

Journal

DAE Symp.Nucl.Phys. 69 (2026)

Date

February 01, 2026

Abstract

We perform the extraction of unpolarized quark TMDs of the proton using the latest FNAL-E-0605 data and the iminuit optimization library.

Summary

This study provides a refined extraction of unpolarized TMDs using modern computational techniques and precision data.

Authors: Abhishek K. P, Reetanshu Pandey, Satyajit Puhan, Suneel Dutt, Harleen Dahiya et al.
