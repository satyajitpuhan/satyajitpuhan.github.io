challenge = "We investigate the T-even transverse momentum-dependent parton distributions (TMDs) for the valence quarks of spin-0 pseudo-scalar mesons (π, K, D, B) up to twist-4 using the light-front quark model. The leading twist TMD f₁ and sub-leading twist TMDs e, f⊥, and g⊥ are calculated. The TMDs are also expressed in terms of parton distribution functions (PDFs) in the collinear limit."
solution = "This work provides a comprehensive analysis of T-even TMDs for pseudo-scalar mesons up to twist-4, offering predictions for the Boer-Mulders and Sivers-like functions within the light-front formalism, and compares with available theoretical and lattice QCD data."
