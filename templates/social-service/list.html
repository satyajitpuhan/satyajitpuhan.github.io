How Satya Seva Works

Three pillars to empower every child's educational journey

📖

Educational Supplies

Providing essential learning materials to children who cannot afford them.

  • Textbooks and notebooks
  • Stationery (pens, pencils, erasers)
  • School bags and uniforms
  • Art and craft supplies
  • Science experiment kits
🎓

Learning Support

Beyond supplies — comprehensive learning support to help children succeed.

  • After-school tutoring
  • Mentorship from researchers
  • Digital learning resources
  • Exam preparation guidance
  • Science and math workshops
🌟

Scholarship Fund

Financial assistance for talented students from underprivileged backgrounds.

  • Merit-based scholarships
  • School fee support
  • Higher education guidance
  • Career counseling
  • Connecting with opportunities

From Odisha to the World & Back

Growing up in a small village in Odisha, I understood firsthand how limited resources can hold back bright minds. My journey from Bhadrak to NIT Jamshedpur, NIT Jalandhar, JINR Russia, PSI Switzerland, and now Academia Sinica Taiwan was made possible by the support of many. Satya Seva is my way of giving back — ensuring that the next generation of curious minds from rural India gets the tools they need to succeed.

Satyajit Puhan
Founder · Satya Seva

Postdoctoral Researcher at Academia Sinica, Taiwan. PhD in Computational High Energy Physics from NIT Jalandhar. With 28 publications in top journals, Satyajit believes that scientific progress and social responsibility go hand in hand. Born in a village in Bhadrak, Odisha — he founded Satya Seva to create the educational opportunities he once dreamed of.
